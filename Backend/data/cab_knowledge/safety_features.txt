PASSENGER SAFETY FEATURES — PROTECTION AND TRUST
==================================================

1. OVERVIEW OF SAFETY FEATURES
   - Cab service platforms invest heavily in safety infrastructure to protect riders and drivers.
   - Safety features operate before, during, and after every ride.
   - The goal is to create a trusted transportation environment where riders feel secure.

2. PRE-RIDE SAFETY MEASURES

   a) DRIVER VERIFICATION
      - All drivers undergo a background verification check before being allowed on the platform.
      - Checks typically include criminal record screening, driving history review, and identity verification.
      - Drivers must submit valid government-issued identification and a commercial driving license.
      - Vehicle inspection and registration verification ensure roadworthy vehicles.

   b) VEHICLE VERIFICATION
      - Each vehicle is inspected for safety compliance (brakes, tires, lights, seatbelts).
      - Vehicle details (make, model, color, license plate) are registered on the platform.
      - Riders can verify the vehicle details before boarding to ensure they are getting into the correct car.

   c) RIDE PIN / OTP VERIFICATION
      - Some platforms require the rider to share a unique PIN or OTP with the driver before the ride starts.
      - This ensures the correct rider is boarding the correct vehicle.
      - The PIN is displayed on the rider's app and must be verbally shared with the driver.

3. DURING-RIDE SAFETY FEATURES

   a) REAL-TIME GPS TRACKING
      - Every ride is tracked in real-time using GPS.
      - The platform monitors the route being taken and can detect significant deviations.
      - Riders can see the live route on their app during the trip.

   b) SHARE MY RIDE / LIVE TRIP SHARING
      - Riders can share their live trip status with trusted contacts (family members, friends).
      - The shared link shows the vehicle details, driver information, and live map tracking.
      - Contacts can monitor the trip in real-time without needing the app installed.
      - This feature significantly enhances rider safety, especially for solo travelers and late-night rides.

   c) SOS / EMERGENCY BUTTON
      - An SOS or emergency button is available in the app during every ride.
      - Pressing the SOS button immediately alerts the platform's safety team.
      - On some platforms, it also directly connects the rider to local emergency services (police, ambulance).
      - The platform shares the rider's live location, trip details, and driver information with responders.
      - The SOS feature is designed for situations where the rider feels unsafe or faces a medical emergency.

   d) RIDE MONITORING AND ANOMALY DETECTION
      - Platforms use algorithms to detect unusual ride patterns (unexpected stops, route deviations, prolonged idle time).
      - If an anomaly is detected, the platform may proactively reach out to the rider via a safety check notification.
      - Riders can respond to safety check prompts to confirm they are okay.

   e) IN-APP COMMUNICATION
      - Communication between riders and drivers happens through the app (masked phone numbers or in-app chat).
      - Personal phone numbers are not shared, protecting privacy for both parties.
      - Call and chat logs are stored for dispute resolution if needed.

4. POST-RIDE SAFETY FEATURES

   a) RATING AND REVIEW SYSTEM
      - After each ride, both riders and drivers rate each other.
      - Low-rated drivers are flagged for review and may be removed from the platform.
      - Riders can report specific safety concerns through the app.

   b) INCIDENT REPORTING
      - The app provides an option to report safety incidents after the ride.
      - Reports are reviewed by the platform's safety team.
      - Serious incidents may result in immediate driver suspension pending investigation.

   c) TRIP HISTORY AND RECEIPTS
      - All trip details are saved in the rider's account (route taken, fare, driver details).
      - This provides a verifiable record that can be used for dispute resolution or safety investigations.

5. SPECIAL SAFETY FEATURES

   a) WOMEN'S SAFETY FEATURES
      - Some platforms offer women-specific safety features such as preferred female drivers.
      - Enhanced safety checks and more frequent ride monitoring for late-night rides.
      - Quick-access helpline numbers within the app.

   b) TRUSTED CONTACTS
      - Riders can set up a list of trusted contacts in the app.
      - Automated notifications can be sent to these contacts when a ride starts or ends.
      - Some platforms allow automatic trip sharing with trusted contacts for every ride.

   c) SPEED MONITORING
      - The platform monitors the vehicle's speed during the ride.
      - If the vehicle exceeds speed limits significantly, a warning may be sent to the driver.
      - Riders are notified if unsafe speeds are detected.

   d) COVID-19 AND HEALTH SAFETY PROTOCOLS
      - Post-pandemic, many platforms implemented health safety features.
      - These include mandatory mask policies, vehicle sanitization tracking, and health self-declarations.
      - Riders can report if a driver is not following health safety guidelines.

6. INSURANCE AND FINANCIAL PROTECTION
   - Many cab platforms provide ride insurance covering accidents during the trip.
   - Coverage typically includes medical expenses for injuries sustained during the ride.
   - Lost item recovery assistance is also available through the app.
   - Riders can file insurance claims through the app's help center.
